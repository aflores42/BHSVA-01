What is Origami?

Origami (折り紙?) "ori" meaning "folding", and kami meaning "paper", is the art of paper folding which is associated with Japanese the culture. The word the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin. The goal is to transform a flat sheet square of paper into a finished sculpture through folding and sculpting techniques.
